Our Story

Why Sam & Co exists

Sam & Co started with a simple observation: the clients who got the most out of consulting weren't buying a framework — they were buying a specific senior person's judgment, and then losing access to that judgment the moment the engagement ended.

We built Sam & Co as the alternative — a collective of practitioners who stay reachable, stay senior, and stay accountable for outcomes rather than deliverables. What started as a handful of partners working informally together across Canada and London became a formal international practice once the pattern was undeniable: clients wanted the team, not just the individual project.

Mission & Values

What we hold ourselves to

Mission

Give leadership teams direct access to senior judgment — not a layer of process between them and the person who actually knows the answer.

Vision

A firm that clients call back not because of a contract, but because the last engagement actually changed how their organization performs.

Values

Trust before selling. Execution before theory. Solutions tailored to the client in front of us, not a repackaged deck from the last one.
