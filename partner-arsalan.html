Biography

Background & experience

Arsalan brings nearly 30 years of advisory experience across Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Public Sector, and Emerging Technology.

Following almost 19 years at Accenture, where he concluded as Executive Director, and earlier tenure at Deloitte Consulting, he has led landmark programs including the PRESTO farecard, a global MedTech ERP transformation, and TD Bank's enterprise integration.

A graduate of Oxford, Cambridge, and MIT, Arsalan specialises in enterprise transformation, ERP implementation, and program recovery, and serves on the boards of Modelomni and Lester Group.
